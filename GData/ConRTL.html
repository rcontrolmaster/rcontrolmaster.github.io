GData ConRTL

Overview

● The ConRTL provides gigabit Ethernet connectivity and power supply for the GData bus system.

● It serves as the central hub for data communication and power distribution to the connected GData modules.

● The ConRTL is designed to ensure reliable and efficient operation of the GData system, making it suitable for industrial inkjet applications that require high-speed data transfer and robust performance.

Module Information

● Transfer rate : 1 Gbps

● Power supply : 24V DC

● Base IP address can be configured via DIP switches
