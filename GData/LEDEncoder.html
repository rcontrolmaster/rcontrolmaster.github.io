GData LEDEncoder

Overview

● The LEDEncoder has three functions:

1. LED stroboscopic lighting controller for drop-watcher.

2. Position encoder sensor signal processing.

3. Trigger pulse generator with programmable period and counter.

Module Information

● Two independent LED stroboscopic lights

● LED voltage : 1 ~ 15 V

● LED current : up to 5 W

● LED strobo delay time and duration time adjustable : 10 ns resolution

● Trigger output for synchronization

● Position encoder input : RS422/RS485 A/B channel

● Encoder counter : 26 bits

● Trigger pulse generator frequency : 0.7 Hz ~ 100 kHz 10 ns period resolution

● Trigger pulse generator counter : 26 bits counter upto 67,108,863

● Trigger pulse generator can produce infinite pulses until stopped by user.
