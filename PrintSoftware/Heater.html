PrintHead Heater Controller

RDriver has a built-in heater controller that can be used to control the temperature of the printhead.
To use the heater controller
1. Enter the target temperature and click "SET"
2. Check the "Heater Enabled" to turn on the heater controller.
3. Check the "AutoRead" in "plotting" to enable automatic temperature reading and plotting.
