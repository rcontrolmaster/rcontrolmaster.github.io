Initialization of RD_SG1024

Enter the IP address and channel number of the printing controller and click "INIT" to initialize the connection.
Automatically detects the driver type, and displays it in the detected device type field.

How to get IP and Channel Information

After successful initialization, waveform settings can be configured for each channel.
Editing the waveform settings allows for precise control over the ink ejection process, enabling optimization of print quality and performance.
Click "download head" to set all the configured waveform settings to the printhead driver.

RD_SG1024_V8 provides 8 independently programmable waveform channels.
Each channel is assigned to a corresponding row of the printhead.
If you checked the "copy this to all" option and click "download head", the settings of the current channel will be copied to all other channels.
