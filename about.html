About RControl

RControl develops driver electronics for industrial inkjet printheads, ink supply units, printing controllers, and printing software.

RControl supports companies adopting inkjet technology by providing self-contained inkjet modules.

RControl has developed numerous devices for FPD manufacturing and continues to expand inkjet technology into new application fields.

History

  • 2012 – Established, developed DPN driver for Trident 256 head
  • 2013 – Developed Dimatix drivers
  • 2015 – Developed integrated RJET driver system for various printheads
  • 2020 – Developed KM1024i drivers
  • 2022 – Developed Ink Delivery Systems
  • 2024 – Developed Test Printing Systems
